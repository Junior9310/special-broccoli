Note: Exact costs are difficult to determine as the government doesn't release complete figures. Estimates are based on:

  • Government Accountability Office (GAO) - Official reports on presidential travel costs
  • Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests - Secret Service and military expense records
  • Air Force One operating costs - Publicly available military data (~$200,000/hour)
  • Secret Service expense reports - Hotel, rental, and equipment costs
  • Judicial Watch investigations - FOIA lawsuit results
  • News investigations - Washington Post, NBC, ProPublica, and others
  • TrumpGolfCount.com - Crowd-sourced tracking of golf outings
